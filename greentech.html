RenewaGreen Technologies

RenewaGreen Solar Panels

RenewaGreen's Solar Panels convert sunlight into useable energy. Solar panels are ideal for homeowner that live in sunny areas.

Benefits of Installing Solar Panels:

  • Solar panels are becoming more afforable
  • Homeowners qualify for tax credits that reduce the cost of installation
  • Save money on the homeowner's utility bill
  • The value of your home will increase
  • Solar panels work in a variety of climates and enviroments

RenewaGreen Wind Turbines

RenewaGreen's Wind Turbines convert wind currents and gusts into useable energy. Wind turbines are ideal for homeowners that live in remote and windy areas.

Benefits of Installing Wind Turbines:

  • Provide and maintain flood control
  • Provides essential backup power source
  • Provides a source of drinking water
  • The value of your home will increase
  • Hydropower systems are becoming more afforable

RenewaGreen Hydroelectric System

RenewaGreen's Hydroelectric System converts the energy of moving water into useable energy. Hydroelectric systems are ideal for homeowners that live nearby or have direct acess to a moving body of water.

Benefits of Installing Wind Turbines:

  • Wind turbines work on different settings
  • Significantly lowers your electricity bill
  • Lowers your utility bills
  • The value of your home will increase

Save Lots of Money by Switching to Renewable Engery


Renewable Electricity Production Tax Credit

Homeowners with renewable technologies installed such as, solar panels, qualify for federal tax credits that help homeowners save more money.

Cut Costs Of Utilities

Homeowners that switch renewable energy resources save an average of $500 per year.

As well, renewable energy systems can pay for themselves over time.

Help The Enviroment & The Planet

Switching to green energy significantly reduces the carbon footprint of your household. Switching to green engery reduces reliance on fossil fuels for energy and decreases the release of harmful pollutants into the atmosphere.
