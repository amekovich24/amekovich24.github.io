RenewaGreen's Go Green Tips For Homeowners

How Homeowners Can Go Green Without RenewaGreen Technology

At RenewaGreen, we recognize that not every home is compatible with our technologies.
Therefore, we created a list of tips homeowners can use to still go green.


Cover Your Windows

Save energy by covering your windows to keep sunlight from adding extra heat into your home.

Don't Use Your Oven

To save energy, cook using your stove, microwave, or outside grill instead of your oven.

Keep The Lights Off

To save energy, keep your lights off when not needed such as exterior and interior lights.

Limit Opening Your Refrigerator & Freezer

Save energy by minimizing the frequency of opening your refrigerator and freezer.

Hang Your Clothes To Dry

Save energy by hanging your wet clothes to dry instead of using your dryer.

Unplug Electronics

Save energy by unplugging electronics when not in use, such as televisions and kitchen appliances.
