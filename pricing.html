RenewaGreen Pricing

RenewaGreen Solar Panels

Solar panels are a great option for a homeowner that lives in a very sunny area.

Our Solar Panels Are Eligible For Tax Credits

RenewaGreen's solar panels are eligible for the Residential Clean Energy Credit, which allows taxpayers who install solar panels to reclaim 30% of the costs through a federal tax credit.

Total Average Cost To Install RenewaGreen Solar Panels

$18,000 through $36,000

Cost depends on your area and size of your hoome's roof.

Solar Panel Installation Cost Per Watt


$2.50 through $3.50 per watt.

RenewaGreen is charging $3.00 per watt.

RenewaGreen Wind Turbines

Wind turbines are a great option for a homeowner that lives in a very windy area.

Our Wind Turbines Are Eligible For Tax Credits

RenewaGreen's wind turbines are eligible for the Residential Renewable Energy Tax Credit, which allows taxpayers who purchase and install a qualifying wind turbines system that is less than 100 kilowatts to receive a federal tax credit.

Total Average Cost To Install RenewaGreen Wind Turbines

$15,000 through $75,000

Cost depends on the amount of kilowatts for the project.

*The average cost is determined by a wind turbine system that utilizes 5 to 15 kilowatts.

Wind Turbine Installation Cost Per Kilowatt


$3,000 through $8,000 per kilowatt.

RenewaGreen is charging $5,000 per kilowatt.

RenewaGreen Hydroelectric System

A hydroelectric system is a greeat option for homeowners living near or with direct access to a moving body of water, such as a river.

Our Hydroelectric Systems Are Eligible For Tax Credits

RenewaGreen is eligible for the Federal Tax Credits for Hydropower and Marine Energy, which allows taxpayers reclaim $5.50 per megawatt-hour and an additional credit of $22.00 per megawatt-hour.

Total Average Cost To Install RenewaGreen Solar Panels

$18,000 through $36,000

Cost depends on the amount the of the hydroelectric system and the amount of watts.

Hydroelectric System Installation Cost Per Watt


In 2022, hydroelectric systems cost an average of $2,881 per kilowatt.

RenewaGreen is charging $2,500 per kilowatt.
